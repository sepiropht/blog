### [The Secret of Our Success - by Joseph Henrich](/books/secret-of-our-success/)

**Date read:** September 2025

How did humans become such a successful species? This book explores how culture, not individual intelligence, has driven human evolution and made us smarter. Henrich demonstrates that our collective brains and ability to learn from each other across generations represent the true secret of our success.

**Publisher:** Princeton University Press | **Rating:** 9/10
